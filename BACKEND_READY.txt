⚠️  BEFORE RUNNING - IMPORTANT:

You need to provide your NEON PostgreSQL database URL:

1. Go to https://neon.tech
2. Create a free PostgreSQL database
3. Copy your connection string (looks like):
   postgresql://user:password@ep-xxxxx.region.aws.neon.tech/auction_portal?sslmode=require

4. Open backend/.env file
5. Replace this line with your actual URL:
   DATABASE_URL=postgresql://user:password@ep-xxxxx.region.aws.neon.tech/auction_portal?sslmode=require
