## Summary of Auction Visual Presence
Every player presented will also show:
- **Team Bidding**: Real-time name and logo of the team currently bidding.
- **Current Bid Value**: Dynamically updating based on increments (e.g., +₹5L, +₹10L).
- **Status Indicator**: Pulse animation for LIVE status.
- **Team Purse Progress**: Visual budget bars for all 10 franchises on the sidebar.
